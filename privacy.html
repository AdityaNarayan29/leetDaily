LeetDaily Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18th, 2026

Overview

LeetDaily is a Chrome extension that helps you track your LeetCode progress, daily challenges, curated study lists (Blind 75, NeetCode 150, LeetCode 75), streak milestones, and coding interview preparation.

What Data We Store

LeetDaily does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

Local Storage (on your device)

The following data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Your LeetCode username and avatar (synced from your logged-in session)
  • Streak count, solved problem IDs, and daily challenge completion status
  • All solve history and progress data

Cloud-Synced Settings (preferences only)

To enable cross-device sync, LeetDaily stores only your settings/preferences on a secure cloud server (Cloudflare Workers KV), keyed by your LeetCode username. This includes:

  • Streak requirement preferences (which lists, topics, and companies you track)
  • Notification settings and reminder times
  • Badge display preferences

No solve history, problem data, passwords, or personal information is ever sent to our server. Only your settings are synced so they appear on any device where you use LeetDaily.

API Usage

LeetDaily communicates with:

  • leetcode.com — to fetch the daily challenge, sync your solved problems and submission history, read your profile stats, and check daily challenge completion. These requests use your existing LeetCode browser session cookies. LeetDaily does not access or store your LeetCode password.
  • leetdaily-prefs.leetdaily.workers.dev — to sync your settings/preferences only (streak rules, reminder time, badge settings). No personal data or solve history is sent to this server.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

We do not use data for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

Your synced settings are stored securely and are only accessible using your LeetCode username.

Data Export & Import

You can export all your LeetDaily data as a JSON file and import it on another device. This is a local file transfer — no data passes through our servers.

Your Control

You may remove all stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Clearing extension storage via Chrome settings
